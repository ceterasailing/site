In another land, with another plan up my sleeve while the other half simpers off in a way.
Common are the stars we wear, fragile in the sense that she wrought, boughten on a day we all ought remember.
When you're lilly white, in and out of spite, you're a member of the offset incumbent array.
Effort in the way you pose. Granted are the pains that we sought.
Runnin' from a day we will oft remember...

Every North swell. Every church bell. See you in the cracks where the memory lapsed, where you would call my name. I'd call upon you.
Mystery sits on a roost of old bones. A relief to be remiss but the twist when you forgot my name. We'll call it payback. Who can see?...

Wrest your soul from a besting of apathy draped in all best regard. Like stars we collapse just to burst from distension, then write off these seasons and focus on heaven...
"Wish yours", says the world to the masses while laying in shade the decision they ask us. They say they want the kingdom but they don't want God in it...

I hesitate to find the words to say to you. My dark side bears this lunar drift 'til i'm blue.
Does your patience thin while chasms careen wide? Am I still lost, doubling down on this doubled mind? You say I've no savior, yeah, you, hand of the king declare me to laze about.
I lay in suffering. So call of the armadas, I'll fall upon that shore and come right on back, baby, yeah, come back for some more.
What do you say?

Delve beyond the frozen seams that render solid memory. Clarity with no reply. It disillusions every eye.
Fare thee well, a forelorn bode the pattern of which, yet, still haunts me.
Distance in the songs you sing between a heart declared awry. Body 'verb had rendered though bestowed upon by what seems farther.
If you still remain for me I'll meet you in your secrecy.
Deeper into the dead of night, your eyes always take me by surprise. Do say you want to. Do say you want to, baby dear.
Some say that dreams are more real than the waking hours and the pain you feel. Do say you want to.
It's already bombs away. Nobody could of warned it'd feel like this.
Did you think one could push so far and coast? You're toast, yeah.
Of all the things that one heart could say. Do say you want. Do say you want to.
Do say you want to, baby dear.
